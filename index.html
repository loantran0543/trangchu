Our Story

A HAVEN OF TRANQUILLITY

Al Moudira is an oasis on the west bank of the Nile in Luxor, near the UNESCO World Heritage site of ancient Thebes and the Valley of the Kings. The hotel is a honeycomb of 10 courtyards, with the feel of a grand country house, embellished with materials rescued from vanishing historic buildings, and surrounded by ten hectares of palm groves and exotic gardens. The timeless inspiration of Zeina Abou Kheir who created the hotel several decades ago, Al Moudira is proudly run by a local team whose charm, style and gentle efficiency have been consistently praised by guests who return year after year.

Cuisine

CUISINE

Cuisine at Al Moudira is deliciously simple. Our chefs source the freshest ingredients from neighbouring farms and create menus that reflect the rich agricultural tradition which has flourished in the Nile Valley for thousands of years. Meals can be served almost anywhere in the hotel, whether in your suite or villa, in the courtyard, garden, pool or at the nightclub.

Wellness

After a day visiting Luxor’s sights, Al Moudira’s Spa is the perfect place to recharge. Reclaimed marble, hand painted tiles, antique basins and traditional bathing rituals form part of a modern wellness programme that encourages both rest and rejuvenation.

Activities

ENGAGE IN ACTIVITIES OR INDULGE IN RELAXATION

GOING OUT

Our team can organize magical bespoke moments, whether you would like to see the sunrise at Karnak or set over the Nile from the deck of a felucca, to visit local artisans, ride a hot air balloon… and almost anything else you may wish to do.

STAYING IN

The gardens, palm groves, orchards, vegetable gardens, and vistas at Al Moudira constantly surprise and enchant visitors. Tobias our Florence-based landscape architect and Taha, with his local team of gardeners, have created an extraordinary oasis, a perfect place to relax beneath the palms, walk among our olive trees, idle away a day in the Spa, enjoy candlelit private dinners.

POOL AND WELLNESS

Relax by the pool.. Surrounded by palms, it is large enough to swim laps, and has a small shallow area safe for young guests. Here you will also find our wellness center where we offer therapeutic massages, signature treatments, sauna and hammam.
